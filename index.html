Emma has been an outstanding athelete all her life. She understands the importance of the support a team can give to an individual to reach certain goals.

Hence the name “Team Emma”. I invite you to join “Team Emma”, and help support Emma as she continues to reach her goal of being cancer free.

Emma's Story

Emma Griffith is a sixteen year old young woman that was diagnosed with stage 4 osteosarcoma in the summer of 2013. She has undergone chemotherapy in California, immunepheresis in Germany, and is soon to start proton beam radiation in Virginia. She continues her fight against cancer and she is steadfast, not shaken, and trusting in the Lord. Psalm 112 : 6-8

Our Goal

Our goal for this tournament is to show our support for Emma and her family. The treatments she is undergoing are costly, so any amount you can give would be appreciated. All net proceeds from the golf tournament and the silent auction will be given to Emma and her family to help with medical and travel expenses.
